Welcome to Maa Maturani Devi Pharmacy College

Maa Maturani Devi Pharmacy College is managed by Maa Maturani Devi Shikshan Sansthan. The College was established in the year 2018 to provide quality education in the field of health sciences. The trustees of the college are already serving the community through other colleges in Ballia. With an aim to promote quality education in pharmaceutical sciences and to fulfil the healthcare needs of society, the institution stands as a beacon of excellence.

The college is recognized by AICTE, PCI New Delhi & Board of Technical Education Uttar Pradesh. It offers two-year diploma programs (D.Pharma) and four-year degree programs (B.Pharma) in Pharmacy, designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the pharmaceutical care sector.

Situated in the natural ambience of lush green fields and mango trees, the college provides an ideal environment for learning. The campus is fully equipped and independently housed, making it one of the most envied and suited locations for an educational institution in Ballia City.
